Every number is now explainable, reproducible, and defensible. We have eliminated data trust as a risk factor, enabling scalable, repeatable capital deployment.

Data Room Loan Sale Tape Engine

An investor-grade loan tape engine that processes 672,701 cells and 183,722 formulas per run across 34 integrated worksheets, producing a 73-field loan-level data tape for the $74.4M Rogue portfolio.
